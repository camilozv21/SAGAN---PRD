User Guide

1. Adding a New Client

  1. Click Clients in the top navigation bar.
  2. Click the + New client button.
  3. Fill in the household information:
    • Client 1 name and salary (required).
    • Check Married to add a second spouse.
    • Set the Transfer day (day of month for automated transfer, usually 28).
  4. Add retirement accounts, non-retirement accounts, liabilities, and insurance policies using the + Add buttons in each section.
  5. Enter the Agreed monthly outflow in the Financial Settings section.
  6. Click Save Client.

2. Generating a Quarterly Report

  1. Navigate to the client's detail page (click their name from the Clients list).
  2. Click + New report.
  3. Set the Report date (e.g., the last day of the quarter).
  4. Confirm or update the Client 1 salary, Client 2 salary, and Monthly outflow.
  5. Enter the current balance and as-of date for every account.
    • Use the Use last button to prefill from the previous report.
    • Check Outdated if you don't have current information (an asterisk will appear on the PDF).
    • For investment accounts, enter the Cash balance if applicable.
  6. Enter the current balance for each liability.
  7. Watch the Live Preview panel on the right to verify totals before saving.
  8. Click Save Report.

3. Downloading PDFs

  1. After saving a report, you'll be taken to the report detail page.
  2. Click Download SACS PDF for the cashflow diagram (2 pages).
  3. Click Download TCC PDF for the net worth diagram (1 page).
  4. Click Download Both (ZIP) to get both PDFs in a single download.

You can also download PDFs from the client detail page under Reports history.

4. Re-downloading an Old Report

  1. Go to the client's detail page.
  2. Scroll to Reports history.
  3. Click View next to the report date.
  4. Download the PDFs from there. Old reports use the original data snapshot — they are never affected by edits to the client.

5. Editing a Client / Fixing Mistakes

  1. Go to the client's detail page and click Edit.
  2. Make your changes and click Save Client.
  3. If a report was already generated with incorrect data, generate a new report with the corrected balances. Old reports remain unchanged as a historical record.

6. Understanding the Numbers

Inflow
Client 1 salary + Client 2 salary
Excess
Inflow − Outflow (transferred to Private Reserve)
Private Reserve Target
6 × Monthly Outflow + Sum of all insurance deductibles
Grand Total Net Worth
C1 Retirement + C2 Retirement + Non-Retirement + Trust
Liabilities
Shown separately — never subtracted from net worth
